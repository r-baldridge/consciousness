## Conclusion

This theoretical framework document establishes the conceptual foundations for implementing plant intelligence systems:

1. **Minimal Cognition**: Functional criteria demonstrate plants meet cognitive thresholds without neurons
2. **Distributed Intelligence**: Plant cognition emerges from distributed, decentralized processing
3. **Embodied Cognition**: Plant intelligence inseparable from plant body and environment
4. **Ecological Intelligence**: Plant cognition extends into ecosystems and communities
5. **Vegetal Philosophy**: Philosophical frameworks reconceive thinking and being for plants
6. **Consciousness Theories**: IIT and GWT potentially applicable to plant systems

These frameworks provide conceptual resources for implementing plant intelligence models that capture the distinctive features of vegetal cognition while remaining grounded in scientific evidence and philosophical rigor.
