# Literature Review: Animal Cognition & Consciousness Research

## Overview
This literature review examines the scientific foundations of animal cognition research, providing the empirical basis for Form 30's comprehensive understanding of non-human minds. Animal cognition studies span comparative psychology, cognitive ethology, neuroscience, and increasingly integrate indigenous knowledge systems about animal intelligence.
