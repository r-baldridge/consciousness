# Altered State Consciousness - Research Applications

## Comprehensive Framework for Meditation-Integrated Consciousness Research

### Executive Overview

This research applications framework integrates traditional contemplative wisdom with cutting-edge consciousness science to establish systematic pathways for investigating altered states of consciousness. The framework encompasses the full spectrum of contemplative practices, from foundational mindfulness to advanced non-dual awareness, while maintaining rigorous scientific methodology and practical therapeutic applications.

### Core Research Philosophy

Our approach honors both the 2500+ year contemplative tradition and modern neuroscience, recognizing that authentic altered state consciousness research requires deep understanding of traditional meditation methodologies combined with sophisticated scientific measurement and validation techniques.
