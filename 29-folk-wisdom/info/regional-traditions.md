## Cross-Regional Themes

### Universal Patterns

1. **Ancestor Veneration**: Nearly universal across all regions
2. **Animism**: Spirit in natural features (mountains, rivers, trees)
3. **Shamanic Practices**: Ecstatic specialists in altered states
4. **Ritual Specialists**: Designated keepers of sacred knowledge
5. **Oral Tradition**: Stories encoding wisdom and ethics
6. **Sacred Geography**: Power places and pilgrimage sites

### Regional Variations

- **Africa**: Strong ancestor focus, vital force concepts
- **Europe**: Fairy/spirit realms, seasonal cycles
- **Asia**: Sky god traditions, shamanic continuity
- **Oceania**: Mana concepts, genealogical focus
- **Americas**: Vision quests, medicine societies
