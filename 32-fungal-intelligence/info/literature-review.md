# Literature Review: Fungal Intelligence and Mycorrhizal Networks

## Overview
This literature review examines the scientific foundations of fungal intelligence, providing the theoretical basis for implementing artificial fungal consciousness systems. Fungal intelligence represents a paradigm of non-neural cognition - the emergence of intelligent behavior from distributed networks without centralized nervous systems, demonstrating problem-solving, memory, learning, and adaptive decision-making through mycelial architectures.
